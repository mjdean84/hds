Home Design Solutions

HDS is a home remodeling company specializing in kitchens and bathrooms. We provide unique designs and upgrades to your needed areas from the ground up. This includes flooring, cabinets, counter and table tops, appliances, and lighting. All of our specialists are kitchen and bath design certified.

We pride ourselves in providing unique solutions for our customers' tastes. For this reason, we keep a limited inventory and get specific products for our specific customers straight from the manufacturer. If you'd like to see more options or product specifications than provided on our site, please contact us or visit our manufacturers' websites.
